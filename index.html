About

Ever wanted to share just the right GIF to express yourself? Well GIFxpression is a web application designed to help you express yourself with GIF's! It uses the GIPHY API to return GIF's that meet your unique expression needs.

To get started, select a rating, then click on a button with the desired expression you want GIF's for! Or better yet, enter your own expression in the input box, and hit enter to make your own button. Then all you need to do is click on it, and see what GIF's pop up! If you like any of the GIF's displayed, click the "copy embed code" to express yourself anywhere on the internet!
